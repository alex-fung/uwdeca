Networking Tips and Tricks

School of Accounting and Finance

The School of Accounting and Finance (SAF) at the University of Waterloo produces outstanding students in the fields of corporate finance, investment management, public accounting, management accounting, and taxation.

In addition to the programs at the undergraduate and Master’s level, SAF’s PhD program produced more accounting faculty than any other program in Canada.
The workshop will explore the do’s and don’ts of networking, tips for personal branding, and ways to maintain meaningful connections.
